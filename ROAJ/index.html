WHAT IS ROA?

Relics of Annorath is a dynamic open world MMORPG. No static skill and class systems.
Knight? Merchant? – Anything you ever wished to be. Proof yourself in this deadly world.

ABOUT US

We, Quantum Bytes Inc., have called Relics of Annorath into life because we want to share more than just a game with you.
Sandboxing at its finest with a realistic first person experience is what we aim for. Our community is an important part of us, providing us constantly with feedback, support and seeing the game come to life step by step.
Being a part of our team, we off er you:
  • INNOVATION
  • BLEEDING EDGE
  • CHALLENGE
We encourage all of our team member to try new skills, deepen old ones and help each other developing themselves.

JOB – 3D Artist

Do you want to envision new worlds? Do you want to be a god of creation? Then we want you!

Creating worlds from scratch and breathing life into it may it be with life forms, objects, houses, greenery – that will be your task.

Skills: Blender, AutoDesk, Animtor, Bones, Blending, Vertex

JOB – Developer

You live for the ultimate performance? The ultimate code? You are a problem solver and finding work-arounds is your thing?

You will be working with a team but also be self-managed in your field of coding.

Skills: C++, Phyton, Git, OOP, Polymorph, Sockets, Debugging
