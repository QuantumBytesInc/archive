Privacy protection

We here at Quantum Bytes take the protection of your personal information very seriously
and adhere strictly to the rules of the data protection acts. We only gather personal
information on our services (webpage, forum, Wiki, Mantis, Relics of Annorath) to the
extent necessary to meet technical requirements. Under no circumstances will the data
collected be sold or provided to third parties for other purposes.

The following statement gives an overview of how we ensure this protection and what
type of data is collected and for what purpose.

  • Data processing for the offered services

    • Quantum Bytes automatically collects and stores information that your browser sends
    to us in its server log files and database. This includes:
    • Browser type/version
    • Operating system
    • Referrer URL (previously visited site)
    • Hostname of the accessing computer (IP address)
    • Time of the server request.
    Quantum Bytes cannot match this data to specific individuals. This data is not merged
    with other data sources.

    Quantum Bytes gathers personal information for services that require a login for access.
    This data is only gathered for purposes of guaranteeing the reliable operation of the
    offered services.

    In addition, Quantum Bytes may use this personal data as legal recourse in the event of
    a violation of the GTC, NDA or other contractual documents that have been concluded
    with the user.

  • Cookies

    • The webpages use so-called cookies at several locations. Their purpose is to make our
    service more user-friendly, effective and secure. Cookies are small text files that are
    stored on your computer by your browser. Most of the cookies we use are so-called
    "session cookies". They are automatically deleted after your visit. Cookies will not
    harm your computer and do not contain viruses.

  • Newsletter

    • If you wish to receive the newsletter offered on our website, we require a valid e-mail
    address and information that allows us to verify that you are the owner of the e-mail
    address or that the owner agrees to receive the newsletter. No additional data will
    be collected.

    You may at any time withdraw your consent to store the data and the e-mail address,
    and for use thereof to send the newsletter.

  • Right to information

    • You have at all times the right to be informed about the data we have stored with
    reference to your person, the origin of the data and its recipient as well as the
    purpose of its storage. Our data protection administrator is responsible for managing
    information on data stored (datenschutz@quantum-bytes.com).

  • Additional information

    • Your trust is important to us, which is why we are always here to answer any questions
    you may have concerning your personal information. If you have any questions that have
    not been answered in this data protection statement or if you wish to have more detailed
    information on a specific issue, please contact our data protection administrator at any
    time (datenschutz@quantum-bytes.com).
